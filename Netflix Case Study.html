How Would You Improve Netflix?

🦾 Goal

To boost Netflix user engagement and subscriber growth, as increased engagement enhances retention, lowers churn, and maximizes lifetime value in a competitive streaming landscape.

About Netflix

Netflix is a global leader in subscription-based streaming, offering a vast library of movies, TV shows, documentaries, and original content. With over 300 million paid memberships across 190+ countries, Netflix is known for its personalized recommendations, high-quality originals, and seamless user experience.

In the next five years, Netflix aspires to solidify its position as the global leader in personalized, inclusive streaming, aiming for 500M+ subscribers through AI-powered personalization, cost-effective tiers, and creator-led discovery. The urgency arises from AI's transformative impact on content curation, mobile-first growth in markets like India (600M+ smartphone users, 80% consuming short videos), and the creator economy's viral reach on platforms like Instagram and TikTok.

✍️

🎖️

Competitor Analysis

Netflix competes with global and regional streaming platforms like Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu, HBO Max, YouTube, JioCinema, and Disney Hotstar (JioStar). Each platform targets user engagement through content, pricing, or unique features.

💪 Strengths
  • Global ScaleMassive subscriber base and presence in 190+ countries.
  • Original ContentHigh-quality exclusives drive brand loyalty.
  • Tech LeadershipSuperior recommendation engine and streaming quality.
😩 Weaknesses
  • Content Licensing CostsHeavy reliance on licensed content increases expenses.
  • Churn in Price-Sensitive MarketsHigh subscription costs deter users in regions like India (South Asia) or LATAM.
  • Content DiscoverySome users struggle to find relevant content despite personalization.
💡 Opportunities
  • Ad-Supported TierAttract price-sensitive users with a lower-cost, ad-supported plan.
  • Interactive FeaturesEnhance engagement with gamified or social viewing experiences.
  • Regional ExpansionDeepen penetration in markets like India with localized pricing and content.
😨 Threats
  • Competitive PressureRivals like Disney+ (JioStar in India) and JioCinema offer lower prices or free tiers.
  • Content SaturationUsers overwhelmed by choice across multiple platforms.
  • Regulatory RisksLicensing and data privacy regulations vary by region.

AI-Powered Chatbot for Content Discovery
P0 — Content Discovery

💡 Idea: Introduce an AI chatbot that users can interact with to find content, receive tailored suggestions, and get short descriptions of shows or movies.

Prototype Demo
Watch on YouTube
Watch on YouTube ·AI-Powered Chatbot for Content Discovery

How It Works

Implement an NLP-based chatbot accessible via the Netflix app or web. Users can ask, "Find me a thriller under 2 hours" or "What's a good Bollywood movie?" The chatbot responds with suggestions and concise summaries (e.g. "RRR: An epic action drama about two Indian revolutionaries, 3 hours, Hindi with subtitles").


Impact

Enhances content discovery for Emma and Priya, reduces choice overload, and increases engagement through conversational UX. Inspired by conversational AI like Grok, ChatGPT or Spotify's AI DJ.

Risks & Challenges

  • Irrelevant Results — May misinterpret queries, reducing trust.
  • Language Gaps — Struggles with regional slang or mixed languages.
  • Integration Issues — Risk of cluttered UI or inconsistent experience.
  • Content Bias — May over-promote popular titles, hurting true discovery.
2
Scene-Based Shorts Creator Program
P0, P1 — Content Discovery & Acquisition

💡 Idea: Enable content creators to access a curated library of pre-approved Netflix clips (15–60 seconds) for posting on Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Facebook, with tools for original audio, AI-generated voiceovers, or subtitles. Partner with influencers in under-penetrated markets to promote shows, driving viral user acquisition and content discovery.

Prototype Demo
Watch on YouTube
Watch on YouTube ·Scene-Based Shorts Creator Program for Content Discovery

How It Works

Build a creator portal within the Netflix app or website, offering a searchable library of clips from popular and back-catalogue content (e.g., Squid Game, Money Heist). Provide editing tools for adding voiceovers (using Netflix's AI dubbing tech), subtitles in multiple languages, and branded "Watch on Netflix" CTAs. Implement DRM and watermarks to prevent piracy. Partner with influencers in key markets (e.g., India, Brazil) for co-branded campaigns, offering revenue-sharing for conversions. Add a "Trending Clips" section in the Netflix app linking to full episodes.


Impact

Drives user acquisition for price-sensitive users like Arjun and Liam by leveraging social media's viral reach (e.g., 400M+ Instagram users in India). Enhances content discovery for Emma and Priya by showcasing diverse titles through creator-driven Reels. Boosts back-catalogue engagement, increasing watch time for older shows.


Example: Reels featuring Disney+'s The Mandalorian draw significant engagement on TikTok, while JioCinema's IPL highlight videos gained millions of views across Instagram.

Risks & Challenges

  • Content Control — Risk of spoilers or out-of-context clips damaging IP.
  • Influencer ROI — Requires careful vetting and performance tracking.
  • Brand Dilution — Overexposure of select scenes may reduce show freshness.

AI-Based Recap Generator (Custom-Length)
P2 — Retention

💡 Idea: Provide AI-generated recaps of previous seasons or episodes, tailored to user preferences (e.g., 1-min or 5-min versions), to reduce drop-off before new seasons.

Prototype Demo
Watch on YouTube
Watch on YouTube ·AI-Based Recap Generator (Custom-Length)

How It Works

Use ML and LLM to summarize key plot points, integrated into the UI before a new season starts. Increases retention by helping users like Emma resume shows, drives watch time. Similar to YouTube fan-made recaps but automated and official.

Risks & Challenges

  • Tone & Emotion Loss — Automated recaps may fail to capture emotional nuance.
  • Language & Localization — Generating recaps in multiple languages while preserving context is complex.
  • Licensing & Talent Approvals — Use of character voices or scenes might require additional legal or actor approvals.
  • User Experience Clutter — Poor placement or overuse could disrupt the app's clean UI.
  • Technical Complexity — Requires reliable scene segmentation, summarization models, and scalable deployment.
5
Advanced Compression Algorithms for Content Delivery
P2 — Streaming Quality

💡 Idea: Use advanced video compression algorithms to optimize streaming quality for low-bandwidth users, offering a 360p mobile-optimized mode for seamless viewing.

How It Works

Adopt codecs like AV1 or VVC (Versatile Video Coding) to reduce data usage while maintaining visual clarity. Introduce a "Mobile-Optimized" 360p mode for users like Arjun in rural areas with unstable networks, with options to toggle quality settings. Use ML to predict network conditions and auto-adjust resolution.


Impact: Improves streaming quality for price-sensitive, mobile-only users like Arjun, reduces buffering, and increases session duration in markets like India and SEA.


Example: YouTube's low-data mode for mobile users.

Risks & Challenges

  • Implementation Complexity — Requires significant backend upgrades and testing across devices and regions.
  • Quality Trade-offs — Aggressive compression may reduce visual fidelity, impacting user satisfaction.

P3 — Family Features

💡 Idea: Introduce advanced parental controls, including a mobile approval lock for adult or sensitive content, requiring authorization from the primary account holder's device.

Prototype Demo
Watch on YouTube
Watch on YouTube ·Enhanced Parental Controls with Mobile Approval Lock

How It Works

Add a feature where adult-rated content (e.g., TV-MA) or sensitive genres on all profiles requires a one-time approval via push notification to the primary account holder's mobile device (e.g. Sofia's phone). Include options for time-based restrictions, content category blocks, and a "Family Hub" with curated, kid-safe content in multiple languages.


Impact: Addresses Sofia's need for robust parental controls, increases family retention, and builds trust in multi-user households.

  • Bypass Risk — Tech-savvy kids may find ways to switch profiles or spoof devices without proper enforcement.

AI-Powered Auto-Dubbing for Contextual Transcreation
P3 — Localization

💡 Idea: Use AI to generate contextually accurate auto-dubbing for all content, ensuring cultural and linguistic resonance across languages (Transcreation of Content — Translational Recreation).

How It Works

Leverage NLP, voice synthesis, and LLMs to create dubs that adapt idioms, humour, and cultural references (e.g. translating Squid Game slang for LATAM audiences). Use ML to match voice tones to original actors and ensure lip-sync accuracy. Offer users like Sofia and Priya a toggle for dubbed vs. subtitled content, with regional language prioritization.


Impact: Enhances localization for Sofia and Priya, improves accessibility, and drives engagement in non-English markets like LATAM, India, and Southeast Asia.


Example: Disney Hotstar's regional dubbing for Indian languages, but automated and scalable.

Risks & Challenges

  • Quality Assurance — Poor dubbing can alienate users.
  • Cultural Nuance — Literal translations may miss cultural humour or idioms.
  • Licensing Barriers — Dubbed rights may need separate licensing in some regions.

Metrics

To measure the success of these solutions in driving user engagement and subscriber growth, the following metrics will be tracked:

📊 User Engagement

Monitor Daily Active Users (DAU), session duration, and titles watched per session to assess interaction frequency and depth.

🔁 User Retention

Track 30-day retention rate and churn rate to evaluate long-term engagement and loyalty.

⭐ Net Promoter Score (NPS)

Survey users to measure satisfaction and likelihood to recommend Netflix, reflecting overall user experience.

🔍 Content Discovery Success

Measure click-through rates on recommendations, chatbot interactions, and search-to-watch conversion to gauge discovery improvements.

💰 Conversion Rate

Track free-to-paid conversions for AVOD users and new subscriber sign-ups to assess acquisition effectiveness.

🏁 Competitive Benchmarking

Compare Netflix's engagement metrics (e.g., watch time, churn) with Disney+, Amazon Prime, and JioCinema to evaluate market position.
